Privacy Policy

Your privacy is non-negotiable

We've designed LivePDF from the ground up to protect your data. Here's exactly what happens to your files.

🛡️

Files are automatically deleted after processing

The moment your processed file is sent to your browser, it is permanently deleted from our servers. We use no persistent storage for user files — everything is handled in memory and temporary buffers.

No file storage
Your uploaded PDF is held in memory only for the duration of processing. It is never written to a database or long-term storage system.
No account required
We collect zero personal information. No email, no name, no phone number. You use LivePDF completely anonymously.
No tracking or ads
We don't run ad networks or tracking pixels. We don't sell data to third parties. This site has no cookies beyond your own dark mode preference stored locally.
Encrypted in transit
All file uploads and downloads are transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your files cannot be intercepted in transit.
Server-side processing only
PDF processing happens on our FastAPI backend. Files are never processed client-side in a way that could expose data to third-party scripts.
